Kadhai Paneer

Description

Kadhai Paneer is a flavorful Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), bell peppers, and a blend of aromatic spices. It is cooked in a kadhai (wok) to enhance the flavors and is often enjoyed with roti or naan bread. The combination of soft paneer and vibrant vegetables makes it a popular vegetarian option.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon kadhai masala (a blend of spices specifically used for kadhai dishes)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add thinly sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.

  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.

  3. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.

  4. Add the thinly sliced green bell pepper (capsicum) and cook for a few minutes until slightly tender.

  5. Add kadhai masala and mix well. Cook for a minute to let the flavors meld together.

  6. Gently add the paneer cubes to the pan and stir them with the masala and vegetables.

  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or naan.Enjoy your Kadhai Paneer!
