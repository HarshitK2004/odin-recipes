Masala Pasta

Description

Masala Pasta Recipe is a simple, delicious, flavorful, and easy-to-make Indian style pasta dish where cooked pasta is tossed in a spicy Indian sauce. This spicy onion tomato sauce has an array of aromatic Indian spices, fresh vegetables, herbs, and some cheese.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Cilantro
  • Fresh Vegetables
  • Oil
  • Pasta
  • Powdered Spices

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt to it. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and cook it according to the instructions on the package until it is al dente.

  2. In a pan, heat oil or butter and saut√© finely chopped onions until translucent.

  3. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.

  4. Add chopped vegetables and cook until slightly tender.

  5. Pour tomato puree or canned tomatoes into the pan, season with spices (red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and garam masala), salt, and pepper. Mix well and let it cook for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  6. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, toss it in the masala sauce, and cook for a few more minutes to combine the flavors.

  7. Garnish with coriander leaves or grated cheese if desired, and serve hot. Enjoy your masala pasta!
